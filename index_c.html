About Us

A Golden Speaker knows what it’s like to start speaking onstage and freeze. But they’ve learned from it. Now they possess the ability to connect with their audience using the right pitch, the right tone, and the appropriate body language.
They are educators, attention-grabbers, crowd benders and effective communicators, all in one package.
A Golden Speaker is driven by purpose. They will call in the middle of the night and ask you to listen to their speech - for the 19th time. If you say it’s perfect, they’ll find someone else. Honest feedback is the fuel to their success, so they listen. Every single day is an opportunity for them to learn and to improve, and they are loving every second of it. Golden Speakers invest in themselves and in others, building a community bound together by encouragement.
One Golden Speaker speaks on behalf of all Golden Speakers. The day of average is finally over. It’s time for a new way to think about Public Speaking. It’s something we like to call: Inspiration Through Speech.
